Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2025

My First Library ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal library management application developed by Emre Sahin. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

My First Library stores all your book and library data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal book data on any external servers.

Advertising

Our free version displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the following data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads:

  • Device Identifiers: Advertising ID (IDFA) for ad targeting and measurement.
  • Usage Data: Information about ad interactions such as clicks and views.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and similar technical data.

You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device's privacy settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking).

Subscriptions

My First Library offers an optional premium subscription to remove ads and unlock all categories. Subscription payments are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Analytics and Crash Data

We may collect anonymous crash reports and performance data to improve app stability. This data does not contain any personally identifiable information.

Data Storage

All your library data (books, categories, reading progress) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have access to this data.

Children's Privacy

My First Library is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. All data remains on the user's device.

Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit: For processing subscription payments. Apple Privacy Policy

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over your data. You can delete all app data by uninstalling the application.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
