Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a book?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, select a category, fill in the book details, and tap Save.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > My First Library > Cancel Subscription.

Is my data backed up?

Your library data is stored locally on your device. It will be included in your iCloud or iTunes backups if you have those enabled.

How do I remove ads?

Subscribe to My First Library Premium to remove all ads and unlock all categories.
